For each pair of photo representing a place where you could travel, select the one you like best. Based on your answers and answers of other users, Octal Travel will suggest 8 places where you should spend your next vacations.

So now, you have a fun way to discover new places you never heared of before through real pictures of what your fellow travelers recommend.

You can also add geotagged photos of places where you've been and they will be presented for rating.

FAQ

Why is the application called Octal Travel?
Because it will suggest you 8 places for your next travels.
How suggestion are calculated?
Suggestions are calculated based on comparative ratings of places represented by photos.
How to add new locations?
You can propose geotagged photos of places where you've been and they will be added on Octal Travel.
